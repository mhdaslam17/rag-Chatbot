Employee Handbook

1. Introduction
Welcome to the company. This handbook outlines the policies and expectations.

2. Dress Code
Employees must follow a professional dress code.
Casual wear is allowed on Fridays.

3. Internet Usage
Company internet should be used for work purposes only.
Accessing harmful or illegal content is prohibited.

4. IT Policy
Employees must not install unauthorized software.
All systems must be secured with passwords.

5. Grievance Policy
Employees can report issues to HR without fear of retaliation.

6. Promotion Policy
Promotions are based on performance, experience, and company needs.

7. Training and Development
Employees are encouraged to participate in training programs.
